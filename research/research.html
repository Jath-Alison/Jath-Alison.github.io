Research

Problem Conception


As competetors in the Vex robotics competition, we felt it was more effort to sort screws back into containers than it was to throw them into a box of random ones and just use fresh screws. In order to preserve resources, money and time, we wanted to create an efficent, easy to use, and cost effective solution to sort screws.

Problem Justification


According to a survey, more than 85% of people who do some sort of robotics say that they have a pile of unsorted screws. 80% of those people say that it will take quite a bit of time to sort all of their screws.
