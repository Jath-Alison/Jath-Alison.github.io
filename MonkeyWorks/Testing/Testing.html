Testing

Product Questionaire


In order to collect data on the effectiveness of a product such as this one, we created a survey to collect information on whether or not it seemed effective, easily useable, producable and convenient.

We also collected data to research further improvements and additions to the product.

This data helped us to gauge the possible success of our product and judge it's viability while also searching for improvement.

The form can be found and filled here

Results


The majority of people felt confident in being able to use our design.
This satisfies our goal of making a product that is effective, yet easy to use.
In order to improve the ease of use, we plan on making the text more readable and the marks more clear, further increasing the usability of the design.

Once again, a large amount of people responded that the design would be produced and distributed.
This shows that we succeded in creating a simple and compact product that can be easily transported or carried for use.
A hole to attatch it to a keychain or other attaching mechanism would allow it to be even more portable and stop it from going missing.


While most said that the product would be effective in helping sort screws, a larger group of 13% argued that it wouldn't.
While the product may not be neccesary or useful for everyone, for the majority of people, it would allow them to identify different screws without the hassle of having to check a ruler or other screws.

Many of the suggestions for improvement came down to readability. The labels became quite messy and hard to read. Aditionally, placing the screw into the slot was a bit tedious and the markers didn't quite match up as much as some had hoped.
We plan to fix this by moving the screw head to be flush with the edge of the product and to clean up the visual aspets a bit to make it more readable.
