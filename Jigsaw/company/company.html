History

We are Jigsaw Robotics, a company that has participated in the BEST Robotics competition since 2009. Throughout the years, our company has worked to impact not only the students on our team, but all members of the community. We wish to provide a platform for employees to grow as leaders, become robotics educators, and understand what it means to collaborate in a professional setting. Our employees range from grades 9-12, from four different Clear Creek ISD high schools. This year, our team has 30 members, with 100% of them planning on pursuing higher education in some STEM field. We work in collaboration with BEST INC. to solve solutions to pervasive issues, such as large-scale manufacturing in our age. Our research during MADE2ORDER has shown us tehcnology can be used to optimize production, and can even prevent extrenuous human labor and expensive company spenditure.

(Jigsaw taking home first place in the notebook and shirt categories and second in the robot category at the North Houston Hub Competition in 2022.)
